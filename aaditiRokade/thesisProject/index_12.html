Visualizing the impact of Climate Change on Flight Experience
An exploration of domestic travel sector of Civil Aviation, India.

One of the causes of disrupted On-Time Performance of India's domestic flights is the weather. Changing trends in parameters contributing to weather especially Temperature & Rainfall impacts the flight experience causing inconvenience to the passengers. This visualization exploires the monthly flight cancellations & passenger complaints for some prominent airline carriers in India's domestic air travel sector based on the temperature and rainfall patterns for years 2014-2018.
